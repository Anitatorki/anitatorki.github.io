About Me

Every day is a chance to explore the depths of the mind and innovate with purpose!!

Hello! I'm Anita Torki, an Information Technology Programming and Analyst graduate from College LaSalle of Montréal. My passion lies at the intersection of Full Stack Developer. I take great pleasure in crafting aesthetically pleasing and high-performance products that provide users with delightful experiences.

As a software engineer with a background in psychology, I excel in tackling complex challenges and pushing the boundaries of what’s possible. My experience spans full stack development (front-end and back-end), machine learning, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and applying psychological principles to technology. I have crafted innovative solutions that blend technical expertise with a deep understanding of human behavior, aiming to bridge the gap between technology and human insight.

In my previous role as an intern at Plumfind, I led the development of a home page and landing page project, focusing on enhancing fashion brands for various companies.


Before Plumfind, I gained valuable experience working for a year at Falat Sanat Ideh in Tehran, where I served as a Full Stack Developer & Data Analyst.

I also worked as a Freelance Frontend Developer at Clinic Mehr from March 2019 to June 2020. During this time, I designed and developed a user-friendly front-end interface for a remote patient monitoring system. I created responsive web pages ensuring seamless access across various devices and screen sizes, designed and implemented a search and filtering system to help users find suitable therapists, created features for scheduling and communication between patients and therapists, and reviewed code and fixed bugs.

Prior to Tehran, I spent 6 years as a Project Coordinator at an Oil refinery in Abadan. Let’s connect to explore opportunities for collaboration or if you’re seeking advanced solutions in software engineering and data science.
